Tostadas De Tinga

shredded smokey chipotle chicken on a tostada with all the fixins

Ingredients

The filling:

  • 1 pound whole boneless chicken breast
  • 4 plum tomatoes
  • 1 ¼ large white onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons canned chipotle
  • 1 teaspoon Mexican oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

The Toppings:

  • Lettuce shredded
  • Sliced Avocado
  • Sour Cream
  • Cilantro
  • Queso Fresco
